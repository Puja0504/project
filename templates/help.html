Overview

Welcome to the Hotel Management System Help Page! This guide will assist you in navigating and using the system's key features.

Room Booking

To book a room, navigate to the booking section. You can choose your room type, check availability, and confirm your reservation.

Guest Management

In this section, you can view, add, and manage guest information, including check-in and check-out details.

Payment & Billing

The payment section allows you to generate invoices, view payment history, and process transactions for guests.

Support

If you encounter any issues, our support team is available 24/7. Please contact us via the support page or email for assistance.
